About the Academy

Building champions in sport & in life — for a generation.

Sport is no longer a pastime — it is an industry, an economic engine, and a vector of national identity. The Integrated Sports City puts Saudi Arabia at the heart of that shift.

Our integrated village combines competition-grade infrastructure with a scientific high-performance core and an international boarding school — giving every athlete on campus the structured, holistic environment required to thrive.

"The Kingdom's most valuable export in the next decade will not be a commodity — it will be the next generation of world-class athletes."

A. Kelany · Founder, Pro Goal Co.

The Athlete Pathway

Eight years from scout to signing.

A structured four-phase journey — from regional trial to professional placement at the Saudi Pro League and beyond.

I
10 – 12 Years

Discover

Regional scouting through school championships and field trials surfaces high-potential athletes from every region.

II
13 – 15 Years

Build

Boarding admission, multi-sport conditioning, international curriculum, and the first layer of performance analysis.

III
16 – 18 Years

Forge

Single-sport focus, internal elite league competition, biomedical monitoring, and tactical classroom integration.

IV
18+ Years

Export

Direct transfer into Saudi Pro League clubs and partner academies across Europe — the commercial output.

Performance & Science

The science that turns talent into titles.

Sensors, footage, and laboratory data converge inside a unified athlete-performance graph — turning every training session into a structured signal that informs the next.

01

AI Performance Graph

Predictive modelling of growth, ceilings, and injury-risk projection — applied to every athlete on campus.

02

GPS & Wearable Telemetry

Live distance, sprint, heart rate, and load metrics streamed from every athlete on every pitch.

03

Video & Tactical Analysis

Frame-by-frame breakdowns, computer-vision positional scoring, and post-match technical review.

04

Biomedical Lab

Continuous metabolic, nutritional, and soft-tissue screening to prevent fatigue-driven injury.
